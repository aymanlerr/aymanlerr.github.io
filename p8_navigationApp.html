Elderly Navigation App Design

This is an Elderly navigation app designed for TOUCH community services. It is designed to be simple to use and elderly-friendly. The app provides public transport as a mode for transport for long distance travel.

Key features:

  • Clear and simple, step-by-step navigation
  • Language support
  • Quick important contacts
  • Bus timing tracker
  • Haptic and volume feedback for reminding elderly of their next destination checkpoint

Software:

  • Adobe XD
